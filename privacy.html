Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 1, 2025

StrideGo is developed and operated by Best Value LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the StrideGo mobile and watch apps.

Information We Collect

  • Health and Activity Data: With your permission, StrideGo reads step counts, distance walked, and heart rate data from Apple HealthKit to display your activity.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymized data such as app crashes, feature usage, and session length to improve StrideGo.
  • Advertising Data: StrideGo may show banner ads. Third‑party ad networks may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads.

How We Use Information

  • To display your steps, distance, and heart rate during workouts.
  • To provide insights and history within the app.
  • To improve app stability and performance.
  • To support limited in‑app advertising.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Limited data may be shared with:

  • Apple Health: StrideGo only reads Health data you allow. We do not write or share this data elsewhere.
  • Service Providers: Analytics and advertising partners may process anonymized data to provide their services.

Your Choices

  • You can control which Health data StrideGo can read in Apple Health → Apps → StrideGo.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in iOS settings: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.
  • You may uninstall StrideGo at any time to stop all data collection.

Data Retention

StrideGo does not store Health data on our servers. Any analytics or crash data is retained only as long as necessary to improve app performance.

Children’s Privacy

StrideGo is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.
