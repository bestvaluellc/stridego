Troubleshooting

Steps aren’t updating in the app

Confirm Motion & Fitness permissions in Settings → Privacy & Security → Motion & Fitness are enabled. On Apple Watch, open the StrideGo watch app once to grant permissions and keep Bluetooth on.

Heart rate isn’t showing during a workout

Start a workout from the watch app and ensure StrideGo has access to Heart Rate in Health → Apps → StrideGo. If it still doesn’t show, end the workout and start a new one.

Ads not appearing

Ads load only with an internet connection and may be limited by availability. Make sure you’re on the latest version from the App Store.
