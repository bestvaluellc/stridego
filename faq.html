Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for iPhone & Apple Watch

Getting Started

How do I start a workout?

On Apple Watch, open StrideGo → tap Start. Swipe to access Pause / Resume / End. Your iPhone will show live stats when nearby.

What permissions do I need to enable?

On iPhone: Settings → Privacy & Security → Motion & Fitness and allow Fitness Tracking. In the Health app: Health → Apps → StrideGo and allow Steps, Walking Distance, and Heart Rate (read).

Live Metrics & Accuracy

Heart rate isn’t showing during a workout

Start the workout from the watch app, not iPhone. Then grant Heart Rate access in Health → Apps → StrideGo. If still missing, end the workout, reopen StrideGo on watch, and start again.

Steps aren’t updating

Confirm Motion & Fitness is enabled on iPhone and that your Apple Watch is connected via Bluetooth. Opening StrideGo on watch once after install helps finalize permissions.

Controls & Settings

Can I disable mile announcements / voice prompts?

Yes. On iPhone open StrideGo → Settings → toggle Voice mile announcements off. (If you don’t see this yet, update to the latest version.)

How do I pause or end a session?

On watch during a workout, swipe to the controls and tap Pause. Tap Resume to continue, or End to finish and save.

History & Charts

My workout appears in history but the 30‑day chart doesn’t update

Charts aggregate by calendar day. If a workout crosses midnight, data may appear on the prior day’s bar. Close and reopen the app after saving a workout to refresh.

How do I filter out workouts with 0 steps?

Use the History filter: Exclude zero‑step entries. (If not present, it’s coming in a future update.)
